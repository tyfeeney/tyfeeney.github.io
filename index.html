About Us

Conflict resolution at a moment's notice

We provide AI driven advice for millions of people around the world struggling with interpersonal conflict. Using state of the art machine learning, we provide comprehensive results for individuals, partners, groups, and organizations. MobileTherapist can help fix marriages or relationship problems, friendships, conflict with coworkers or managers, and so much more.

Download the app

Download the MobileTherapist app from the App Store or Google Play.

Complete a short survey

For every new interpersonal issue, we ask the user to complete a short questionare. The survey will ask you about the type of issue and the duration of time conflict has occured. You can provide optional information, such as your social media profile and the public social media profiles of involved parties. For optimal results, we recommend that you include as much relevant and specific information as you can.

Press "Listen"

On the home screen, press the "Listen" button. Once you allow MobileTherapist to access your microphone, MobileTherapist will be able to record and analyze your interactions. If you want to stop recording audio at any time, press the "Stop Listening" button.

Look for the "Results" button

Once we have enough data to access your case, a "Results" button will appear. You will be given the option to select an individual session or group session, and add more participants if needed. Although you can choose whichever mode you prefer, we sometimes recommend specific session options.

If you decide to link any third-party social media apps to MobileTherapist, you also agree to allow us to use information from these accounts, including any public posts and data from your friends and contacts.

We collect data from your mobile network and your device. Whenever you use MobileTherapist, you agree to share any log data from your device.
