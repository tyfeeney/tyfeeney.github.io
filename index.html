Privacy Policy

Information you provide

By signing up for an account on the MobileTherapist app, you agree to share with us your name, personal email address, phone number, internet IP address. By allowing us access to your microphone, you also agree to allow us to collect audio from your surrounding environment. You also agree to share any additional information collected by survey, including information given about third-party individuals. If you decide to link any third-party social media apps to MobileTherapist, you also agree to allow us to use information from these accounts, including any public posts and data from your friends and contacts.

Information from your device

We also collect data from your mobile network and your device. Whenever you use MobileTherapist, you agree to share any log data from your device. Log data may include times and dates of access, your IP address, activities on the MobileTherapist app, and requests for mobile therapy. We also use session and persistent cookies to store information on your IOS or Android device. Cookies help us store your language and app settings (such as therapist appearance) for future use. We also store information about the make and model of your phone or tablet, as well as its operating system.

Providing personalized services

We are committed to provide you with the best and most comprehensive treatment possible. As a result, we use survey and audio interaction data to provide you with personalized assistance. Any optional information you provide is used to improve MobileTherapist services for other users.

Analytics

By sharing information about your device and network, you agree to share analytic data with us. Our analytic engineering team uses your device data to fix bugs in the MobileTherapist mobile client, as well as improve app performance and user experience.

Sales and Advertising

We also use your personal data to advertise medical over-the-counter drugs and hospitals, and independent medical practices. Although we partner with medical institutions, MobileTherapist does not provide legal medical advice. For more information about professional medical treatment, please see your primary care physician.

Delete Your Account

When you delete your account, data that is personally linked to your account will be deleted.
